What we do?

We are a adventure/trekking group in Maharashtra. Also a member of MAC (Maharashtra Adventure Council). We officially started operating back in 2015 and have organized more 300 events so far. We are not just into organizing treks but we also organize camping, waterfall rappelling, river rafting, backpacking tours and much more. Our team is committed towards your safety and overall good experience.

WHERE YOU GO IS THE KEY

Trekking with us is different

Do you go through this? You find yourself stuck in a situation that doesn't allow you time for adventure. The lack of excitement in your daily life leaves you restless. This is where our treks make a difference. Our outdoor leaders conduct sessions that weave mindfulness into your journey. In the lap of the mountains, they engage in thought-provoking exercises that help you reflect and contemplate. Very often, the person you are before and after a trek is different. Our leaders teach you new outdoor skills that give you the confidence to trek alone anywhere in the world. Our treks are transformative journeys.
